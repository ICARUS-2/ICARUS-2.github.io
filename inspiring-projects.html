RTX is nVidia's latest addition to its long line of PC graphics cards. Based on nVidia's own Turing architecture, the technology behind these cards allows for real-time ray tracing, making images and reflections clearer and more detailed than ever. These cards come in several models: The RTX 2060, 2060 Super, 2070, 2070 Super, 2080, 2080 Super, and finally the most powerful (and expensive) model: The 2080TI.

Released in 2016, Overwatch is Blizzard Entertainment's latest team-based first-person shooter. The game places two teams of 6 against each other in a fight to capture objectives and move payloads. With a diverse cast of 32 heroes with many abilities, there are endless possibilities when it comes to strategizing to win the game.

Serato DJ Pro is a professional program used by DJs to do live mixing and track blending. It can use up to four audio channels and is often used with external hardware such as software controllers, turntables, and CDJ players. The software is upgrade-ready with expansion packs that allow users to connect digital vinyl systems (DVS) as well as video playback devices to enhance performances

As PCs get more and more powerful, they tend to generate a lot more heat from components such as the CPU and GPU, especially when overclocking. For PCs that cannot be cooled properly using heatsinks and fans, watercooling is a viable solution. A watercooling system consists of a combination of pumps, tubing, and radiators in order to disperse heat more effeciently and quietly than standard air coolers.
